MUSHROOM MASALA


INGREDIENTS

  • Fresh Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Tomatos
  • Cashews
  • Spices
  • Dry chillies
  • cumin seeds
  • Fresh cream
  • Cooking oil
  • Ginger and Garlic paste
  • Corriander leaves

      • PROCEEDURE

      Lets start our cooking by the following steps

      1. Chop the onions,tomatos,chillies,
      2. Take the chopped tomatos,chillies and cashews into the blender
      3. Blend the mixture into a nice slurry without any lumps
      4. In the mean time,take a pan , heat and pour sufficient oil
      5. Add the required spices along with ginger garlic paste
      6. saute them for a minute
      7. Add the chopped onions and let them fry for 10 more minutes
      8. Add the blended mixture along with chopped mushrooms
      9. stir and Adjust salt and Spice
      10. Let them cook for thirty minutes
      11. Turn off the stove and Garnish with cream and chopped corriander leaves
      12. Enjoy with Chapathi,Rice or With a ButterNaan
